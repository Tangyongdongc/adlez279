# Style Guide
Subset of X-SAMPA to be used for song transcriptions. If the original singer's pronunciation differs from these guidelines, prioritize accuracy of matching the vocals.
## Vowels
Phoneme | Description | Example words | Notes
--- | --- | --- | ---
`A` | open back unrounded vowel | cot `k_h A t_}`
``A` `` | rhotacized open back unrounded vowel | bar ``b A` ``
`{` | near-open front unrounded vowel | bat `b { t_}`
`e@` | diphthong of close-mid front unrounded vowel to schwa | and `e@ n d`<br>jam `dZ e@ m` | May sound like `{` or `E` depending on context and the singer's accent
`E~` | nasalized open-mid front unrounded vowel | bank `b E~ N k` | May sound like `{` depending on the singer's accent
`V` | open-mid back unrounded vowel | but `b V t_}`
`Q` | open back rounded vowel | ball `b Q 5`
`O` | open-mid back rounded vowel | bowl `b O 5`
``O` `` | rhotacized open-mid back rounded vowel | pour `p_h O`
`@` | schwa | about `@ b aU t_}` | Most vowels reduce to this in short, unstressed syllables
`E` | open-mid front unrounded vowel | end `E n d`
``e` `` | rhotacized close-mid front unrounded vowel | bear `b e`
`3` | open-mid central unrounded vowel | bird `b 3 d`
`I` | near-close front unrounded vowel | bin `b I n`
`I_~` | nasalized near-close front unrounded vowel | ping `p I_~ N` | Allophone of `I` before `N`
`1` | close central unrounded vowel | woman `w U m 1 n` | High vowels reduce to this in short, unstressed syllables
`i` | close front unrounded vowel | bean `b i n`
``i` `` | rhotacized close front unrounded vowel | beer ``b i` ``
`U` | near-close back rounded vowel | push `p U S`
``U` `` | rhotacized near-close back rounded vowel | poor ``p U` ``
`u` | close back rounded vowel | boom `b u m`
`aI` | diphthong of open front unrounded vowel to near-close front unrounded vowel | bye `b aI`
`eI` | diphthong of close-mid front unrounded vowel to near-close front unrounded vowel | bay `b eI`
`OI` | diphthong of open-mid back rounded vowel to near-close front unrounded vowel | boy `b OI`
`oU` | diphthong of close-mid back rounded vowel to near-close back rounded vowel | no `n oU` | May sound like `@U` depending on the singer's accent
`aU` | diphthong of open front unrounded vowel to near-close back rounded vowel | how `h aU`
`aU_~` | nasalized diphthong of open front unrounded vowel to near-close back rounded vowel | pound `p aU_~ n d` | Allophone of `aU` before nasal consonants
`5_=` | syllabic velarized alveolar approximant | pull `p 5_=`

## Consonants
Phoneme | Description | Example words | Notes
--- | --- | --- | ---
`p` | voiceless bilabial plosive | spin `s p I n` | In consonant clusters
`p_h` | aspirated voiceless bilabial plosive | pat `p_h { t_}` | Beginning of syllable
`p_}` | unreleased voiceless bilabial plosive | cup `k_h V p_}` | End of syllable
`b` | voiced bilabial plosive | bun `b V n`
`t` | voiceless alveolar plosive | stick `s t I k_}` | In consonant clusters
`t_h` | aspirated voiceless alveolar plosive | tubs `t_h V b z` | Beginning of syllable
`t_}` | unreleased voiceless alveolar plosive | pat `p_h { t_}` | End of syllable
`d` | voiced alveolar plosive | dawn `d Q n`
`k` | voiceless velar plosive | scan `s k e@ n` | In consonant clusters
`k_h` | aspirated voiceless velar plosive | cup `k_h V p_}` | Beginning of syllable
`k_}` | unreleased voiceless velar plosive | stick `s t I k_}` | End of syllable
`g` | voiced velar plosive | go `g oU`
`tS` | voiceless postalveolar affricate | church `tS 3 tS`
`dZ` | voiced postalveolar affricate | jump `dZ V m p`
`4` | alveolar flap | little `l I 4 5_=` | Generally between 2 vowels where the second is unstressed. May sound like `t`, `d`, or `?` depending on the singer's accent
`f` | voiceless labiodental fricative | fun `f V n`
`v` | voiced labiodental fricative | love `l V v`
`T` | voiceless dental fricative | think `T I N k`
`D` | voiced dental fricative | that `D { t_}`
`s` | voiceless alveolar fricative | see `s i`
`z` | voiced alveolar fricative | zoo `z u`
`S` | voiceless postalveolar fricative | show `S oU`
`Z` | voiced postalveolar fricative | vision `v I Z @ n`
`h` | voiceless glottal fricative | home `h oU m`
`C` | voiceless palatal fricative | heat `C i t_}` | Allophone of `h` when preceding `i` or `j`
`?` | glottal stop | uh-oh `V ? oU`
`m` | bilabial nasal | month `m V n T`
`n` | alveolar nasal | none `n V n`
`N` | velar nasal | song `s Q N`
`l` | alveolar lateral approximant | love `l V v` | Beginning of syllable
`5` | velarized alveolar approximant | ball `b Q 5` | End of syllable
`r` | alveolar approximant | run `r V n` | Written as `r\` in standard X-SAMPA but abbreviated here for English only transcription
`j` | palatal approximant | you `j u`
`w` | labial-velar approximant | what `w V t_}`
